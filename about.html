About Me

Personal Details

Hey, my name is Apurva Pandey and I live in Mumbai, a beautiful seaside city in the west coast of India. I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in textile design at the National Institute of Fashion Technology Mumbai, India.

I have always been passionate about design, and as a young girl, sketching and doodling my ideas for textile designs was my favourite hobby. However, after my school education, I was forced to take up engineering under severe family pressure. I continued pursuing my interest in design informally, and after 2 years, having decided that my true calling lies in the fashion industry, I obtained admission in the premier fashion school in the country.

NIFT Mumbai has given me the facilities and mentorship to pursue my passion and given me training in different areas of textile design. I am experienced in print design, weaving, textile processing, embroidery, surface development, printing and illustration, and proficient in Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, NedGraphics, Sketch and CorelDRAW. My portfolio will be able to give you an idea about my thoughts and ideas in design, and is a testament of my skill set. Specific areas in the fashion industry that interest me are print design, merchandising and marketing, and I hope to make a career in this fast paced dynamic industry.
